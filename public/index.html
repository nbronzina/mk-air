why ephemeral?

Because not everything needs to be saved. In a world obsessed with archives and endless playback, mk-air brings back the beauty of things that happen once—and then disappear.

Your voice, a track, a passing thought—shared live, with whoever's there to hear it. And when it's done, it's gone.

No pressure to polish. No need to perform. Just the freedom that comes from knowing it's temporary.

how it works

  1. click "start broadcast" and allow mic access
  2. share your unique stream link with anyone
  3. they listen live while you're broadcasting
  4. when you stop, it's gone forever

what to broadcast

jam sessions ambient field recordings late night thoughts practice sessions sonic experiments morning coffee moments poetry readings whatever feels right
